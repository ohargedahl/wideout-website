What makes Grunda different

Workouts that adapt

Grunda generates workouts based on your current level — slow flex, fast flex, reverse kegels, staircase holds, pyramid sets — with hold and rest durations calibrated to your ability.

Progress you can see

Earn XP, level up, and build streaks. Shields protect your progress when life gets in the way. A "Grounded Wellness" design that feels calm, not clinical.

Science-backed

Every interval and progression rule is grounded in pelvic floor physiotherapy research. No guesswork — just structured training that works.

Local-first and private

Your training data lives on your device. No accounts, no cloud sync, no tracking. Just you and your progress.
