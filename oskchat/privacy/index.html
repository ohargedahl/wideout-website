Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Summary

OskChat is designed to respect your privacy. Your workout data, training history, and coach conversations are stored on Supabase servers under your authenticated account. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

Account and authentication

OskChat offers Sign in with Apple, email/password, and Google Sign-In. When you sign in:

  • Sign in with Apple: we receive a unique identifier and, optionally, your name and email. Apple may provide a private relay email address.
  • Email/password: we store your email and a hashed password. We never store passwords in plain text.
  • Google Sign-In: we receive your Google ID token for authentication. We do not access your Google account data.

Data we store

OskChat stores the following data on secure servers (Supabase, hosted in the EU):

  • Your profile information (name, training preferences)
  • Workout history, exercise logs, and training plans
  • Chat messages with the AI coach
  • Goals, progress data, and program enrollments
  • Coach memory (things the AI remembers about your training)

All data is scoped to your authenticated user account via Row Level Security. Other users cannot access your data.

AI-powered features

OskChat uses AI (via OpenAI) to generate personalized workouts, coaching advice, and training insights. When you use these features:

  • Your messages and training context are sent to our secure proxy server, which forwards them to OpenAI for processing.
  • The proxy runs as a Supabase Edge Function with server-side rate limiting and authentication.
  • AI requests are logged for rate limiting and cost control. Logs are automatically cleaned after 30 days.
  • All communication is encrypted in transit.

OpenAI's data usage policy applies to the processing of these requests. See OpenAI's privacy policy.

Analytics

OskChat uses TelemetryDeck to understand how the app is used in aggregate. TelemetryDeck is privacy-focused by design:

  • No personal identifiers are sent. Anonymous hashed identifiers rotate periodically.
  • No IP address logging, no cookies, no cross-app tracking.
  • Analytics data is aggregated and used only to improve OskChat.

See TelemetryDeck's privacy policy for details.

Crash reporting

OskChat uses Sentry to capture crash reports and fix bugs. Crash reports include device type, iOS version, and a stack trace. They do not include personally identifiable information. Chat messages, workout data, and AI prompts are explicitly stripped from crash reports before they are sent.

Subscriptions

If you subscribe to OskChat Pro, payments are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. Subscription status is managed by RevenueCat, which stores an anonymous identifier tied to your Apple device. We do not receive or store your payment information.

See RevenueCat's privacy policy.

Data we do NOT collect

  • Your contacts, photos, location, or microphone
  • Health data from Apple Health (unless you explicitly enable HealthKit integration)
  • Payment information (handled entirely by Apple)

Your rights

Under GDPR and other applicable laws, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You can delete your account and all associated data from the app's settings. For questions about data we may hold via TelemetryDeck, Sentry, or RevenueCat, contact us below.

Children

OskChat is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes

If we update this policy, the revision date above will change. Material changes will be announced in the app.
